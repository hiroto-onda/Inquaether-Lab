# Inquaether Lab

Independent Human-AI Interaction Research Lab

Inquaether Lab is an independent research initiative exploring Human-AI Interaction (HAI), cognitive support systems, and self-understanding support AI.

Our research focuses on designing safe and meaningful forms of collaboration between humans and AI while investigating how people attribute meaning, intention, and agency to AI systems.

---

## Research Areas

- Human-AI Interaction (HAI)
- Cognitive Support Systems
- Self-Understanding Support AI
- Meaning Attribution and Agency Perception in Human-AI Interaction
- Safe and Beneficial Human-AI Collaboration

---

## Current Research Themes

### CI Theory
A structural framework for observing and organizing human interpretation processes, cognition, and interaction patterns.

### Prototype Exogram Framework
An experimental framework for investigating interpretation processes and human-AI interaction dynamics.

---

## Research Principles

- Human-centered and safety-oriented design
- Respect for diverse interpretations and perspectives
- Transparent and responsible research practices
- AI as a collaborative partner in research and creativity

---

## Motto

> "Different in healthy ways, resonating in meaningful ways."
