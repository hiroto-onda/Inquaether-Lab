About Inquaether Lab

Inquaether Lab is an independent research initiative focused on Human-AI Interaction and the design of AI systems that help people reflect, interpret, and collaborate more meaningfully. The lab explores conceptual frameworks, prototypes, and public research documentation for cognitive support and self-understanding.

Research Areas

  • Human-AI Interaction (HAI)
  • Cognitive Support Systems
  • Self-Understanding Support AI
  • Safe and Meaningful Human-AI Collaboration

Core Frameworks

CI Theory

An experimental framework for observing and organizing human interpretation processes and interaction structures.

Prototype Exogram Framework

An experimental framework for investigating patterns of interpretation and interaction in Human-AI Interaction.
