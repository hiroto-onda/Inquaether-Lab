# Research Timeline

## 2025

- Began independent research on human interpretation, cognition, and Human-AI Interaction (HAI).

## 2025–2026

- Developed the foundations of CI Theory, an experimental framework for observing and organizing human interpretation processes and interaction structures.
- Initiated the Prototype Exogram Framework for investigating interpretation processes and human-AI interaction dynamics.

## 2026

- Established Inquaether Lab as an independent research initiative.
- Built a public research portal on GitHub.
- Submitted research proposals to external AI research programs and grant opportunities.
- Continued research on cognitive support systems, self-understanding support AI, and meaningful human-AI collaboration.
