# Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

## Are you attempting to create consciousness?

No.

The research focuses on observable patterns of interpretation and interaction and does not assume consciousness as a solved phenomenon.

## Are you making metaphysical or spiritual claims?

No.

The research adopts an observational and structural approach and does not make claims regarding metaphysical entities or supernatural phenomena.

## Is this research intended to replace human judgment?

No.

The objective is to support human understanding, cognitive support systems, and beneficial Human-AI collaboration.

## Are the public documents the complete research?

No.

The public documentation provides high-level overviews of the research. Unpublished materials, implementation details, experimental notes, and private repositories are intentionally excluded.

## Why focus on interpretation and interaction?

Interpretation and interaction are observable phenomena that may contribute to a better understanding of Human-AI Interaction and the development of supportive AI systems.
