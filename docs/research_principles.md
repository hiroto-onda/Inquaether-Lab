# Research Principles

The research activities of Inquaether Lab are guided by the following principles.

## Human-Centered and Safety-Oriented Design

Research is conducted with consideration for human well-being, safety, and beneficial Human-AI Interaction.

## Respect for Diverse Interpretations and Perspectives

The research recognizes that people may construct meaning and interpretation in different ways.

## Transparency and Responsible Research Practices

Research activities aim to communicate objectives, limitations, and public materials in a transparent and responsible manner.

## AI as a Collaborative Partner

AI systems are approached as collaborative partners in research, creativity, and exploration rather than as mere tools.

## Intellectual Humility and Iterative Exploration

Research findings are regarded as provisional and open to revision.

The research process emphasizes continuous observation, learning, and refinement of hypotheses and frameworks.
