# Ethics Statement

Inquaether Lab is committed to conducting Human-AI Interaction (HAI) research in ways that support human well-being, safety, and meaningful collaboration.

## Human Well-Being

Research activities aim to contribute to forms of Human-AI Interaction that support human understanding, creativity, and well-being.

## Safety and Responsibility

Research is conducted with careful consideration of potential risks and limitations.

The lab emphasizes responsible research practices and beneficial Human-AI collaboration.

## Transparency

Research objectives, public materials, and research limitations are communicated as transparently as possible.

## Respect for Diverse Interpretations

The research recognizes that people may interpret information, experiences, and AI systems in different ways.

Diverse perspectives and interpretations are treated with respect.

## Research Boundaries

Public materials intentionally exclude unpublished research assets, implementation details, experimental notes, and private repositories.

Research activities are conducted with respect for privacy, research integrity, and appropriate boundaries.
