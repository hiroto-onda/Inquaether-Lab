# Research Highlights

## Inquaether Lab

Independent Human-AI Interaction Research Lab

## Research Areas

- Human-AI Interaction (HAI)
- Cognitive Support Systems
- AI Systems that Support Self-Understanding
- Safe and Meaningful Human-AI Collaboration

## Core Frameworks

### CI Theory
An experimental framework for observing and organizing human interpretation processes and interaction structures.

### Prototype Exogram Framework
An experimental framework for investigating patterns of interpretation and interaction in Human-AI Interaction.

## Research Principles

- Human-centered and safety-oriented design
- Respect for diverse interpretations and perspectives
- Transparency and responsible research practices
- AI as a collaborative partner
- Intellectual humility and iterative exploration

## Current Status

- Independent research initiative
- Public research portal established
- Ongoing documentation and framework development
- Continuing research on Human-AI Interaction and supportive AI systems
