# Mission Statement

Inquaether Lab is an independent research initiative dedicated to exploring Human-AI Interaction (HAI), cognitive support systems, and AI systems that support self-understanding.

The lab seeks to investigate patterns of interpretation and interaction in ways that contribute to safe, meaningful, and beneficial collaboration between humans and AI.

The research emphasizes observation, structural understanding, intellectual humility, transparency, and respect for diverse perspectives.

The long-term objective is to contribute to forms of Human-AI Interaction that support human understanding, creativity, and well-being.
