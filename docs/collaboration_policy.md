# Collaboration Policy

Inquaether Lab is open to thoughtful discussion and collaboration related to Human-AI Interaction (HAI), cognitive support systems, and AI systems that support self-understanding.

## Areas of Interest for Collaboration

- Human-AI Interaction research
- Cognitive support systems
- AI systems that support self-understanding
- Research communication and educational materials
- Safe and meaningful Human-AI collaboration

## Communication Principles

Collaboration and discussion are approached with respect, transparency, and intellectual humility.

The lab values diverse perspectives and constructive dialogue.

## Research Boundaries

Public collaboration does not include access to private repositories, unpublished research assets, implementation details, experimental notes, or confidential materials.

Public materials provide high-level overviews and research directions.

## Research Support and Inquiries

Inquaether Lab welcomes opportunities for research support, grant discussions, educational collaboration, and interdisciplinary dialogue where appropriate.

All collaboration should respect research integrity, safety, privacy, and appropriate boundaries.
