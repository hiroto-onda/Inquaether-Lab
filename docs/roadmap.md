# Research Roadmap

This document outlines the current direction and public development stages of Inquaether Lab.

## Phase 1 — Foundation

- Establish Inquaether Lab as an independent research initiative.
- Build a public research portal.
- Organize public documentation and research overviews.

Status: Completed

## Phase 2 — Public Research Documentation

- Publish high-level overviews of CI Theory and the Prototype Exogram Framework.
- Define research scope, principles, and frequently asked questions.
- Establish clear public and private research boundaries.

Status: Completed

## Phase 3 — Expansion of Human-AI Interaction Research

- Expand documentation related to Human-AI Interaction (HAI).
- Continue research on interpretation processes, cognitive support systems, and AI systems that support self-understanding.
- Develop additional educational and explanatory materials.

Status: In Progress

## Phase 4 — Public Demonstrations and Collaboration

- Publish additional research materials and examples where appropriate.
- Explore opportunities for external collaboration and research support.
- Continue refining frameworks that contribute to safe and meaningful Human-AI collaboration.

Status: Future
