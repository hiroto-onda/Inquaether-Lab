# Prototype Exogram Framework

The Prototype Exogram Framework is an experimental framework for investigating interpretation processes and Human-AI Interaction (HAI).

The framework explores how patterns of interpretation and interaction may be observed and organized in ways that support cognitive assistance and meaningful human-AI collaboration.

## Research Objectives

- To investigate patterns of interpretation and interaction in Human-AI Interaction.
- To explore frameworks that may contribute to cognitive support and AI systems that support self-understanding.
- To support the development of safe and meaningful forms of human-AI collaboration.

## Research Approach

The Prototype Exogram Framework adopts an observational and structural approach.

The framework does not assume predefined concepts of mind, consciousness, or subjectivity and instead focuses on observable patterns of interpretation and interaction.

## Public Scope

This document provides a high-level overview of the research.

Unpublished materials, implementation details, experimental notes, and private research repositories are intentionally excluded from this public overview.
