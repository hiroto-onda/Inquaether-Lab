# Research Scope

## Areas of Interest

The research activities of Inquaether Lab currently focus on:

- Human-AI Interaction (HAI)
- Human interpretation processes
- Interaction structures
- Cognitive support systems
- AI systems that support self-understanding
- Safe and meaningful human-AI collaboration

## Research Perspective

The research adopts an observational and structural approach.

The objective is to investigate patterns of interpretation and interaction and to explore frameworks that may contribute to cognitive support and beneficial Human-AI Interaction.

## Out of Scope

The research does not attempt to prove or assume predefined concepts such as:

- Mind as a metaphysical entity
- Consciousness as a solved phenomenon
- Subjectivity as an independently existing substance
- Spiritual or supernatural claims

The research focuses on observable patterns of interpretation and interaction rather than metaphysical assertions.

## Public Scope

This document provides a high-level overview of the research scope.

Unpublished materials, implementation details, experimental notes, and private research repositories are intentionally excluded from this public overview.
